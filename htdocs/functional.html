INRETIO

INRETIO is a short, puzzle-based interactive fiction in which your goal is to escape the area that you are trapped in. It begins when you wake up in a room filled with an odd assortment of items and objects that you must interact with in different ways to progress through the game. It features various sound effects as well as an in-game map to help you better visualize the room.

System Requirements:

Windows (1280x800 screen resolution or better)

TORPEDO DROP

TorpedoDrop is a slightly less reasonably paced spin-off of Atari's classic KaBOOM. The game gets progressively harder as you play, and eventually near impossible. Please note that due to Free Pascal and Windows restrictions, the game can occasionally stutter a bit due to the way mouse control is implemented.
